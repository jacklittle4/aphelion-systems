Process

A build path that keeps decisions visible.

The process is designed to turn uncertainty into working software without letting the project become vague, bloated, or hard to finish.

Method

From idea to launch.

Each phase produces something concrete: a direction, a prototype, a build, a refined product, and a handoff.

  1. Align

    Map the offer and audience.

    Clarify goals, users, actions, tools, content needs, and launch constraints.

  2. Design

    Shape the interface.

    Create the page structure, visual direction, and prototype for feedback.

  3. Build

    Develop the working product.

    Implement responsive pages, app behavior, forms, integrations, and content.

  4. Refine

    Test what people will notice.

    Review mobile layouts, loading speed, accessibility basics, and form behavior.

  5. Launch

    Ship and support the handoff.

    Deploy, connect analytics, document the system, and plan the first improvements.

Standards

A few things every build should respect.

Clear before clever

The design should make the offer easier to understand first.

Fast enough to feel effortless

Pages should load quickly and avoid effects that get in the way.

Editable after launch

Content patterns and handoff notes should leave room for growth.

Built around real workflows

Apps and automations should match the way the team already works.
