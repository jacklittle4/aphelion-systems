Scope clarity

What affects the price.

A few factors move a project up or down from its starting point.

Page count

More pages mean more layout, content structure, and review. A one-page launch costs far less than a five-page site.

Custom features

Calculators, booking flows, quote tools, and interactive screens add design and build time beyond static pages.

Integrations

Payments, CRMs, scheduling, admin dashboards, and automations connect to outside systems and need testing and handoff.

What every project includes

You are paying for more than a template. Each build covers the planning, implementation, and launch details that cheap site builders skip.

  • Responsive design, basic SEO, form setup, deployment, and a launch review
  • Clear handoff notes and direct communication with the person building it
  • Not included by default: paid domain, hosting, email, and stock-photo subscriptions
  • Common extras: copywriting, extra pages, logo work, advanced integrations, and monthly reporting
