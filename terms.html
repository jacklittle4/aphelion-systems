Terms of use.

Plain notes that keep early projects clear. Formal terms can be expanded later.

The short version.

This site is informational. It explains what we do and roughly what it costs, nothing more. Anything you read here is a starting point for a conversation, not a commitment.

Quotes are estimates.

Prices shown on the site or given in early conversation are estimates, not contracts. Real numbers depend on scope, and we confirm them before any work starts.

Scope is agreed in writing.

Project specifics are agreed in writing per project. A written scope covers the pages, features, timeline, price, revisions, and handoff expectations so both sides know what is being built.

  • Larger projects may need a deposit before work begins and final payment before handoff or launch.
  • Domains, paid hosting, paid email, analytics, and payment processors are separate, client-owned services where needed.

Ownership transfers on final payment.

Code and finished project files belong to you once the project is paid in full, unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

Questions.

If anything here is unclear, write to aphelionsystems.dev@gmail.com and we will talk it through before we start.
