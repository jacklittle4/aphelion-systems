Terms

Plain project expectations.

Formal terms can be expanded later, but these notes keep early projects clear.

Scope

Each project should have a written scope covering pages, features, timeline, price, revisions, and handoff expectations.

Payment

Larger projects may require a deposit before work begins and final payment before handoff or launch.

Ownership

After full payment, finished project files belong to the client unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

Third-party services

Domains, paid hosting, paid email, analytics accounts, and payment processors are separate client-owned services if needed.
