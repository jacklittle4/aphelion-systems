The technical setup

Domains, hosting, payments, and shipping — handled.

The pieces that usually trip up a small business are part of the job, set up in your own accounts and in your name, with no marked-up middleman fees.

Domains

I register and configure your domain, point the DNS to your site, and explain renewals so it never lapses. It stays in your name, never held hostage, with no markup on the registration fee.

Hosting

Fast, reliable hosting matched to your site — from free static hosting for a marketing page to a managed server for a full app. Deployed, tested, and documented so you always know where things live.

Payments

Accept cards online through a secure, hosted checkout set up in your own account. Low per-sale fees, no complicated compliance burden, and a test transaction to confirm it works end to end.

Shipping

Shipping zones, rates, and discounted carrier labels configured for your store, including free-over-threshold rules and a full test order before launch.
