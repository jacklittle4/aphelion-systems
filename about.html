About

A one-person web and software studio.

Aphelion Systems is the solo studio of Jack Little, built around a single idea: small businesses deserve big-budget work, priced fairly and supported personally.

Where the name comes from.

An aphelion is the point in an orbit farthest from the sun. The name is a nod to my background in engineering physics, which is also where the technical side of this work comes from.

Aphelion is run by Jack Little, a full-stack software engineer and engineering physics student. The studio exists to give small businesses, creators, and startups the kind of work that usually carries a big-budget price tag, without the agency overhead and without settling for a template.

You get sharp digital tools that are genuinely affordable, the personal attention of working directly with the person writing the code, and a partner who thinks about where your business is headed, not just where it is today.

  • Custom websites, modern frontends, forms, and dashboards.
  • Automations and practical tools built for how you actually work.
  • A build that starts small and scales into custom software only when custom software is the right answer.
