About

A small web studio for practical business systems.

Aphelion Systems is built around a simple idea: small businesses, creators, and startups should be able to get sharp digital tools without paying for layers of agency overhead or settling for a template.

Human presence

A direct build process with one accountable developer.

Aphelion is run by Jack Little, a developer focused on custom websites, modern frontend systems, forms, dashboards, automations, and practical tools for small businesses and creators.

The advantage is direct communication and careful scope. You are working with the person building the site, so decisions about layout, forms, launch, ownership, and future updates stay clear.

The work starts with the smallest useful version, then scales into custom software only when custom software is actually the right answer.
