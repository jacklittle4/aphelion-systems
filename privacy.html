Privacy

Simple privacy policy.

Aphelion Systems uses contact forms to understand project inquiries and reply to potential clients.

Information collected

Forms may collect your name, email, business name, website, project goals, budget, timeline, and preferred contact method.

How it is used

Information is used to respond to inquiries, scope projects, prepare quotes, and provide project support.

Analytics

No analytics tool is currently installed. If Google Analytics, Plausible, or Cloudflare Web Analytics is added later, this page should be updated.

Third-party forms

Forms are handled through FormSubmit while the site is hosted for free on GitHub Pages.
